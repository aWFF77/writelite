Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What We Collect

Grammar checking data: Text you write is sent to DeepSeek API solely for grammar analysis. Text is NOT stored, logged, or retained after processing.

Settings: Extension preferences are stored locally in your browser. They never leave your device.

Usage data: A local counter of daily grammar checks is stored in your browser. It never leaves your device.

What We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect browsing history, email content, personal information, or use cookies/tracking scripts.

Third-Party Services

Grammar processing: DeepSeek API (platform.deepseek.com/privacy). Payment processing: Paddle (paddle.com/privacy).

Data Security

All API communications use HTTPS encryption.
